Frequently Asked Questions

About Octa/General

What is Octa?

Octa is a web based and mobile app channel that helps you invest and save simultaneously. With Octa you get up to 12% interest on your investment and XXX% on your savings. Octa helps you invest and save towards a goal or target.

How do I start investing and saving with Octa?

To begin investing and saving, download and register on the app or our website and fund your investment account.

How do I set up an investment account?

Click “+ Invest” on the dashboard or menu tab, provide the required information. You can also view the tour guide to see steps for each action.

What document do I need to register for octa?

You don’t need any document. You only need the app, your BVN and your card details to enjoy the services of Octa.

Why are you requesting for my BVN?

CBN requires you to have a BVN to own a fully functional account. We need your BVN to automatically gather your personal information, so you won't need to fill any form when signing up.

What kind of investing product is Octa?

Octa is a regular investment product which comes with a wallet. The wallet automatically becomes your savings account and you can earn interest on both your investment(s) and savings accounts. The interest earned on your investment account is periodically sent to your savings account (wallet) from which you can pay into your bank account or perform other transactions and bills payments.

Why am I seeing Page account balance in Octa?

When you open an investment account with Octa, you are automatically assigned a wallet which operates as a Page International Financial Services savings account. Page International Financial Services Limited is our financial partner and parent company duly licensed by the CBN.

Why use Octa?

Octa is a smart solution that helps you invest your money and simultaneously save the earned returns on your investment. With Octa, smart digital investment and savings, you earn above bank interest rate on your investments and you have access to collateral free loans while also enjoying a smart way to save towards a target or goal with your investment(s) and savings accounts.

How much does it cost to use the Octa app?

Using the app is completely free. There are no hidden charges, maintenance fee, sms charge or the sort. You get #ZeroCharge with Octa.

Savings and Interest

How much can I start with?

You don’t need millions to start investing. With Octa, you can invest as little as N1000 and be on your way to smart financial growth.

What is the interest rate?

You earn up to 13% as interest on your investment and up to XXX% on your regular savings (wallet).

Can I set up more than one investment?

Yes, you can. You can create different investments for different goals and even give each one a unique name for easy recall.

Can I lock my account?

Yes, you can lock your investment for any specific time. You can also leave your savings unlock. Withdrawals are at Zero charge.

How long is maturity?

That is entirely up to you. However, keep in mind that the longer you invest, the more your interest is. It is best to roll-over funds to enjoy compound interest.

How safe is my money?

Your money is very safe. All funds are housed by Page International Financial Services Limited, a registered and license retail financial service provider in Nigeria.

What is the deal between Octa and Page Financials?

Page International Financial Services is the holding company of Octa. Page International Financial Services is a licensed financial institution in Nigeria, so Octa is fully covered and regulated.

Are my card details safe?

Yes, your card details are very safe, we work with Remita to process all payments.

How do I buy data and airtime with Octa?

This process is like the bill payment process. Log into the app, get to payment tabs, click data/airtime recharge and input the value to make payments.

The app won’t open, what do I do?

Kindly close the app and reopen at after two minutes.

The APP suddenly closes, what do I do?

Click the icon on your device again and it will reopen.

I have forgotten my password

Click the reset password link and follow the steps.

Why do I have an account number?

You are given an automatic account number with our parent company Page International Financial Services which is a fully licensed financial service provider.

What if I don’t have funds in my account?

We will try to deduct from your source account twice. If deduction is not possible, it would be left, and regular deductions carried out later.

Can I access a loan?

Yes, one of the benefits of Octa is that you have better access and chances of getting a loan.

What other benefits can I get with Octa?

We are always evolving and looking for ways to better serve you. Once we have a new offer, be sure you’ll be the first to know.
